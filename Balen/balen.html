About Balen

From rapping to reforming - A new kind of leadership for Nepal

Balendra 'Balen' Shah is a Nepalese rapper, structural engineer, and politician serving as the Mayor of Kathmandu since 2022. Born in 1990 in Kathmandu, Shah represents a new generation of leaders who have entered politics from non-political backgrounds, bringing fresh perspectives and innovative approaches to governance.

Shah rose to fame as a rapper with his hit song 'Nepali Rapper' and used his platform to address social issues and political corruption. In 2022, he ran as an independent candidate for Mayor of Kathmandu and won by a landslide margin of 87%, defeating established political parties.

As Mayor, Shah has focused on waste management, urban development, transparency, and citizen participation. His administration is known for its tech-savvy approach, social media engagement, and direct communication with citizens, breaking traditional bureaucratic barriers.

The Journey

From engineering to rapping to political leadership

2010s

Engineering Career

Worked as a structural engineer after completing education. Developed professional expertise in construction and urban planning that would later inform his political work.

2018

Musical Breakthrough

Released 'Nepali Rapper' which became an anthem for youth. Used music to critique social issues and political corruption, gaining widespread popularity and becoming a voice for change.

2022

Historic Election

Ran as independent candidate for Mayor of Kathmandu. Won by unprecedented 87% margin, defeating established political parties. Campaign focused on transparency, waste management, and youth empowerment.

2022-Present

Transformative Leadership

Implemented innovative waste management systems, improved urban infrastructure, and established direct citizen engagement through social media. Became symbol of new political possibilities in Nepal.

Key Reforms

Transforming Kathmandu through innovation and transparency

Waste Management Revolution

Implemented smart waste collection systems, increased recycling, and cleaned historic areas. Reduced waste backlog by 70% in first year.

Urban Infrastructure

Improved roads, drainage systems, and public spaces. Launched 'Smart Kathmandu' initiative focusing on sustainable urban development.

Transparency & Accountability

Established real-time project tracking, open budget portals, and weekly public meetings. Set new standards for municipal governance in Nepal.

Youth Engagement

Created youth advisory councils, internship programs, and innovation labs. Empowered young people in decision-making processes.
